Cookie Policy

This policy explains how Mentify uses cookies and similar storage technologies to provide a smooth, secure, and customized experience.

Last updated: July 2026 • Scope: Web Browsers & Sessions

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your browser or device when you visit websites. They help the platform remember your login state, theme preferences, and security settings.

2 How We Use Cookies

Mentify uses cookies exclusively for core site operations:

  • Essential Session Cookies: Keep you logged in securely as you navigate between dashboard pages, video lessons, and assignments.
  • Security & CSRF Tokens: Protect forms against cross-site request forgery attacks.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your selected display mode (light/dark theme) and consent preferences.

3 No Advertising Tracking

We do not sell data or use third-party cookies for commercial advertising tracking.

4 Managing Your Preferences

You can manage or clear cookies directly through your browser settings. Please note that disabling essential cookies may impact your ability to remain logged in.
